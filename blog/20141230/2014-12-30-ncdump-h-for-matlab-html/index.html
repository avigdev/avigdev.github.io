ncdump -h for matlab

December 30, 2014 at 01:16 PM | categories: matlab, octave | View Comments

I launch "ncdump -h" many times during my workflow. It gives you all the meta-data you need for netcdf files, without the hassle of openning a more serious program like ferret . I figured out that I need the same for mat files. You will need octave to make it work… 

1: #!/usr/local/bin/octave -q
2: whos('-file',argv(){1})
